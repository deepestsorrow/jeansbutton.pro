Cookie Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are text files placed on your computer or device to collect standard internet log information and visitor behavior. When you visit our portfolio, we may collect information regarding your interaction with our GSAP-driven galleries through cookies or similar technology.

2. How We Use Cookies

SDAP uses cookies in a range of ways to improve your experience on our website, including:

  • Ensuring smooth scroll performance and asset loading for high-resolution macro photography.
  • Understanding how you navigate our hardware catalog.
  • Remembering your interactions with our corporate B2B inquiry forms.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies: Required for the fundamental operation of our digital infrastructure.

Analytical Cookies: These allow us to recognize and count visitors and see how they navigate our structural interfaces to optimize the global viewing experience.

4. Managing Cookies

You can adjust your browser policies to decline cookies. However, disabling all cookies may cause some smooth-scrolling animation features to degrade gracefully.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about our Cookie Policy, please contact our administrative team at privacy@jeansbutton.pro.
