Manufacturing Policies

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

To maintain peak quality control and machine calibration standards, SDAP enforces a strict Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ) of 5,000 units for standard catalogue hardware, and 15,000 units for fully bespoke 5-axis CNC molds.

Prototypes and pre-production sampling are available exclusively to registered brand partners undergoing the onboarding process.

Tolerances & Quality Assurance

All structural denim hardware is engineered to a mechanical tolerance of ±0.01 mm. Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) and molecular anti-oxidation coatings are guaranteed to withstand class-4 industrial enzyme and stone-wash cycles without structural degradation.

Intellectual Property & Non-Disclosure

We respect the absolute confidentiality of our partners' designs. All bespoke mold schematics and engraved logo dies are strictly protected under mutual NDA and destroyed or securely vaulted upon collection termination.
