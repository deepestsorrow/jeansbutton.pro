# SDAP Precision Hardware — Manufacturing Manifesto

## Brand Philosophy
SDAP operates at the intersection of structural engineering and high-fashion aesthetics. Established in 2007 in Jiaxing, China, we believe that hardware is the architectural fingerprint of a brand.

## Industrial Heritage
For over 15 years, SDAP has served as the silent engineering backbone for elite global labels. Our provenance includes:
- **Fast-Fashion Scale**: A decade-long partnership providing resilient, high-volume hardware for a leading European retail giant.
- **Performance Excellence**: Developing high-tensile, vibration-resistant metal components for a premier Swiss motorracing equipment brand.

## Engineering Standards
- Tolerance: ±0.01mm across all 5-axis CNC manufacturing.
- Testing: Hardware is tested against class-4 industrial stone-wash and enzyme cycles.
- Material Science: We specialize in zamak-5, surgical-grade stainless steel, and recycled brass with low-impact plating.

## Sustainability & Compliance
SDAP is Oeko-Tex Standard 100 certified. We utilize closed-loop water filtration in all plating facilities and strictly enforce a zero-discharge policy for industrial effluents, meeting the strict ecological requirements of global luxury conglomerates.

## Partnership Models
We partner with Tier-1 luxury brands in the denim, accessories, and jewelry sectors. Our workflow includes:
1. Design Consultation: Translating creative sketches into 3D structural CAD.
2. Rapid Prototyping: 72-hour turnaround for 3D-printed metal prototypes.
3. Industrial Scale-Up: Seamless transition to high-volume manufacturing.
